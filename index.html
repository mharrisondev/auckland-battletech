About Us

We’re a community of Classic Battletech enthusiasts in Auckland who meet weekly at HobbyMaster in Penrose Auckland to play every Tuesday; from ~5:30pm till closing at 10pm.

We play the Classic rule set of Battletech, using the Classic rule set

( Battlemech Manual / Total Warfare. ) A quick-start sample of the Battletech rules can be found on the Official Website

You don't need to own any miniatures, books, or supplies to come along. We have spare 'Mechs, record sheets, maps, and dice — everything you need to play your first game. We're happy to teach the rules and run demo games for newcomers of any experience level.

If you've played the MechWarrior video games, the HBS BattleTech PC game, or just love giant stompy robots, you'll feel right at home. Classic Battletech is the original tabletop game that inspired them all.

How to get in contact

All club communications are within the AMC (Auckland Model Citizens) Facebook group, feel free to join!

Motivation

I created this simple website for search engine optimisation.

When I grew an interest in picking up Classic Battletech as a hobby, I had a remarkably difficult time finding a group within Auckland. The majority of table-top hobby groups organize themselves within Facebook groups, often private groups; which can be difficult to find and have poor search engine optimisation. This is my attempt to get some visibility on Google and point people whom are interested in the right direction.
